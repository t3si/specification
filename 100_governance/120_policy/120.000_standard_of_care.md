# 120 Standard of Care

## 1.0 Purpose
This directive defines the minimum technical and operational requirements (The Standard of Care) for all entities operating within the T3S Markov Blanket. It transforms subjective "Beliefs" into objective **Technical Booleans**.

## 2.0 Core Assertions (The Verification Gates)
To maintain the Standard of Care, every system artifact and agentic action must satisfy the following assertions:

### 2.1 Assertion of Structural Integrity (A0)
* **Requirement:** Every artifact must strictly adhere to the **Anchor and Leaf Protocol** and **130.000 Manifest Schema**.
* **Boolean Gate:** `verify_schema(artifact) == TRUE && matches_recursive_index(artifact.filename) == TRUE`

### 2.2 Assertion of Provenance (A1)
* **Requirement:** No artifact shall exist without a cryptographically signed parentage and a verified `steward` declaration.
* **Boolean Gate:** `exists(artifact.signature) && verify(artifact.signature) == TRUE`

### 2.3 Assertion of Intent (A2)
* **Requirement:** Every automated action must be semantically linked to a URN via the **JSON-LD Knowledge Graph** mapping back to the 100-block Strategy domain.
* **Boolean Gate:** `artifact.json_ld.intent_urn != NULL && resolve_urn(intent_urn).status == ACTIVE`

### 2.4 Assertion of Locality (A3)
* **Requirement:** Sensitive system logic and identity data must reside within the "Local-First" sovereign boundary unless an explicit "Cloud-Second" waiver is attached.
* **Boolean Gate:** `artifact.storage_class == 'Sovereign' || exists(artifact.cloud_waiver) == TRUE`

### 2.5 Assertion of Temporal Integrity (A4)
* **Requirement:** All evidence generated (500-block) must be timestamped and anchored to the specific version of the Governance block (100) active at the time of execution.
* **Boolean Gate:** `artifact.timestamp.governance_version == active_version_at_runtime`

### 2.6 Assertion of State Fidelity (A5)
* **Requirement:** The physical state of the infrastructure (**400-block CMDB**) must be reconciled against the Normative State defined in the Governance block (100).
* **Boolean Gate:** `compare(actual_state, normative_state) == MATCH`

## 3.0 Violation Protocols
Any artifact or process that returns `FALSE` on any Core Assertion is considered "Non-Normative."
* **3.1 Automated Halt:** In high-hazard blocks (300/400), a `FALSE` result must trigger an immediate pipeline failure or agentic suspension.
* **3.2 Forensic Logging:** All failures must be recorded in the **500-block (Evidence Database)** and mirrored to the **600-block (Observability)** for human-in-the-loop remediation.

## 4.0 Governance
The Standard of Care is the primary technical mandate of the **T3SI**. It is subject to quarterly peer review to reflect emerging regulatory requirements in FinTech, EHS, and critical infrastructure sectors.
