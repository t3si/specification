# 020 Version Control and Lifecycle Manifest

## 1.0 Purpose
This manifest provides the **Service Transition** record for the T3S framework. It tracks the evolution of the standard from a collection of research notes into a formal, ITIL-aligned governance system, ensuring a deterministic audit trail of all structural modifications.

## 2.0 Versioning Schema
T3S utilizes a modified Semantic Versioning (SemVer) approach:
* **MAJOR:** Fundamental shifts in the **010 Mandates** or Markov Blanket logic.
* **MINOR:** Structural additions to the Observational Plane (100-900 blocks).
* **PATCH:** Clarifications, typographical corrections, or leaf-level updates.
