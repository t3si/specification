# 500 Technical Operations and Service Evidence

## 1.0 Purpose
This block serves as the **Evidence Database (EDB)** for the system. It records operational telemetry, service logs, and forensic evidence of compliance with the 010 Mandates.

## 2.0 Objectives
* **Objective D:** Secure operational data within the sovereign layer.
* **Objective E:** Provide verifiable evidence for insurability and audit.

## 3.0 Current Status
**Genesis.** Leaf directories in this block will house immutable logs and service transition evidence.
